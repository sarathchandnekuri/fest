The Best Bits of the Vintage Asian Fan Fest

The Vintage Asian Fan Fest is an all-day event brought to you by FANFEST. Combining the educational depth of a conference with the lively atmosphere of a festival, our event is designed to be both informative and fun.

Expect inspiring talks from experts in Asian art, interactive fan-making workshops, cultural performances, engaging exhibit tours, and a warm, welcoming community. Whether you're here to deepen your knowledge, connect with fellow enthusiasts, or simply enjoy the beauty of vintage fans, our festival has something for everyone.

Join us for a memorable day that celebrates the artistry and history of Asian fans, and leave with new skills, new friends, and a deeper appreciation for this elegant craft.
